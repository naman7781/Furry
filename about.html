About The Dog People

Founded in 2020, FurryFur is the world's largest network of 5-star pet sitters and dog walkers.



Bienvenue!!!

In need of a keeper for your pets? Then you have come to the right place.

Whether it is restrictions of routine, unexpected calls of work, an escape from hectic urban life, or keeping a promise to attend that anniversary party, we are here to help you with your pet needs.

Mans N’ Rover is a digital service platform that helps pet owners find suitable service providers to take care of their pets.

There are over 9.9 million dogs and 10.9 million cats in UK, totalling 20.8 million which is a huge number. We aim to provide various pet owners with a range of services they can rely on with their pet needs viz a viz pet walking, sitting, boarding and grooming.

We are here to help you provide your beloved FurryFur with a happy holiday home, where it’d be nurtured and nourished. How can you be sure ?

It’s our job! (And, we love it.)

“To connect pet-parents with pet-lovers.”

OUR BUSINESS PLAN

Our business is based on the primary premise of connecting you with the best pet-keeper available in your locality. Our technology rests on a firm algorithmic footing which rates service providers on a variety of factors and generates the most suitable match for your pet-keeping and grooming needs. We emphasise on pet-safety and transparency of service while striving to make it more affordable, streamlined and easy to use.

Our team loves its job i.e. “To connect pet-parents and pet-lovers.”

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Our primary focus is to connect you with the most suitable service provider while ensuring the safety and well-being of your best friend. We know how difficult it can be to depart from your FurryFur, so we list wellness of your pet on our main agenda not on the side lines.
